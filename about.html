About Me

Douglass Stanton

I instantly fell in love with programming as soon as I began. I have an unquenchable thirst for learning and improving, which makes development the perfect thing for me! There will always be something more to learn, and there will always be new technologies. I particularly enjoy developing web apps with plenty of functionality and user interaction. I enjoy the challenge of working out all the logic within an app, and figuring out how to handle data.


I began my programming journey several years ago. I started off slow, learning the basics with HTML and CSS as a hobby after work. When I started learning JavaScript it changed from a hobby to a passion, and then to a career goal. Nowadays I spend the majority of my time studying and practicing web development.


I live with my fiance and two cats near Albany, NY. We moved here from northern Kentucky, where we lived for 3 years. My fiance and I both grew up in Pennsylvania, but in different parts of the state. We met in college at Mercyhurst University where I studied Russian and Mandarin while playing for the hockey team.
